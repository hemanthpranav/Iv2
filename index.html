Interaction Reference

  • Dropdown Filters: Filters all visualizations by selected criteria
  • Click Bar (Manufacturer): Filters views to show vehicles from that manufacturer
  • Brush in Scatter Plot: Selects vehicles within the brushed area
  • Click Point/Box: Selects specific vehicle(s) across views
  • Reset Button: Clears all filters and selections
